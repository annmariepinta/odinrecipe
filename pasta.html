Spaghetti Recipe

A rich and satisfying pasta dish perfect for lunch or dinner. This version of spaghetti features a savory tomato sauce, herbs, and your favorite protein or veggies.

Preparation time

  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional: meatballs, vegetables, cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti: Cook in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© aromatics: Heat oil in a pan. Add onion and garlic; cook until soft.
  3. Add sauce: Pour in tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and simmer.
  4. Combine: Toss the spaghetti in the sauce. Add meatballs or veggies if using.
  5. Serve: Garnish with grated cheese or herbs and serve hot.
