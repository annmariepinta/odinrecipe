Amala and Ewedu Recipe

A beloved Nigerian delicacy from the Yoruba cuisine, Amala served with Ewedu and stew offers a rich, earthy taste and smooth texture â€” a must-try for traditional food lovers.

Preparation time

  • Total: 35 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups yam flour (Elubo)
  • 2 cups fresh Ewedu leaves (or frozen)
  • 1 teaspoon ground crayfish
  • 1 teaspoon potash (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ground melon (optional)
  • Meat or fish stew to serve

Instructions

  1. Prepare Amala: Boil water and gradually stir in yam flour until thick and stretchy. Stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  2. Cook Ewedu: Boil the Ewedu leaves with potash for 5 minutes. Blend or whisk until smooth.
  3. Season: Add crayfish, melon, and salt. Simmer for a few more minutes.
  4. Serve: Dish out Amala with Ewedu on the side, and pour stew with meat or fish on top.
