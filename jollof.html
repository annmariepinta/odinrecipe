Nigerian Jollof Rice Recipe

A beloved West African dish, Nigerian Jollof Rice is rich, smoky, and spiced with tomato, pepper, and traditional herbs. It’s a classic party favorite.

Preparation time

  • Total: 50 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 1 Scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt, thyme, curry powder, and seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. Blend the sauce: Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, and scotch bonnet into a smooth paste.
  2. Sauté onions: Heat oil and fry onions till fragrant. Add tomato paste and cook.
  3. Add blended mix: Pour in blended pepper mix and cook till reduced.
  4. Season and add rice: Add seasoning, stock, and washed rice. Stir well.
  5. Simmer: Cover with foil and lid. Cook on low heat until rice is tender and fluffy.
