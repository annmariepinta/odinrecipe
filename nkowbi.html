Nkwobi Recipe

Nkwobi is a rich and flavorful Eastern Nigerian delicacy made from tender cow foot pieces drenched in a spicy palm oil sauce, perfect for adventurous tastebuds!

Preparation time

  • Total: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 kg cow foot (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 cup palm oil
  • 1 teaspoon potash (kaun)
  • 2 tablespoons ground pepper
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • Utazi leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook cow foot: Boil cow foot with salt, onion, and seasoning cubes until very tender.
  2. Prepare sauce: Mix potash with a little water. Add palm oil and stir until thick and yellow (emulsified).
  3. Add spices: Stir in crayfish, pepper, and salt into the palm oil mix.
  4. Combine: Add the cooked cow foot into the mixture and stir until evenly coated.
  5. Garnish and serve: Serve warm in a wooden bowl (optional) and garnish with sliced onions and utazi leaves.
