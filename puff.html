Puff Puff Recipe

Puff Puff is a classic West African snack — golden, fluffy, deep-fried balls of dough that are sweet, soft, and perfect for any occasion!

Preparation time

  • Total: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Rising: 45 minutes
  • Frying: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast: Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix batter: In a bowl, combine flour, salt, and nutmeg. Add the yeast mixture and stir into a smooth, thick batter.
  3. Let it rise: Cover and leave in a warm place for 45 minutes to 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Fry: Heat oil in a deep pan. Scoop batter in small balls and fry until golden brown.
  5. Drain and serve: Remove from oil, drain on paper towel, and enjoy warm.
