Chicken Salad Recipe

A refreshing, healthy, and satisfying salad featuring tender grilled chicken, crunchy vegetables, and a creamy dressing â€” perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

Preparation time

  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts (sliced)
  • 1 head lettuce (chopped)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/2 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 boiled eggs (sliced)
  • Dressing of choice (e.g., ranch, vinaigrette)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients: Wash and slice all vegetables. Grill and slice chicken breasts.
  2. Assemble salad: In a large bowl, mix lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, corn, and carrots.
  3. Add protein: Top with sliced chicken and eggs.
  4. Dress and season: Drizzle with your favorite dressing and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately chilled or at room temperature.
